When viewed through the lens of *ductus* (the movement of the pen), Model 2 describes **Departure Angles**. 

- **✅ Strong Fit (Dynamic)**: C, G, J, Q, S, U, O, L, Z. (L and Z are structural fits for their specific bearings).

## Visual Style Requirement
1. **Ductus Priority**: Letters must be formed by movement vectors rather than static construction.
2. **Hemispheric Contrast**: Physical letters (South) should be "heavier" or more curved; Intellectual letters (North) should be sharper and more "staccato."
3. **The Southwest Axis**: The primary slant and weight stress should align with the 249° (SW) "S" axis for maximum harmonic resonance.
